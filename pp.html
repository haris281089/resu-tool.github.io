Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 2, 2024

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you explicitly enter into the input fields of the AI Resume History Optimizer tool. This includes:

  • Personal Identifiers: Your Name and Gender Preference.
  • Employment History: Job titles, companies, tenure, core responsibilities, and quantified metrics/achievements.
  • Education Details: Degrees/certifications and institutional names.
  • Target Role Data: The Target Company Name, Hiring Manager Name, and the full text of the Job Description (JD).

2. How Your Information is Used

The information you provide serves two primary purposes:

  • AI Processing: Your history and the Target JD are combined into a single, comprehensive prompt and sent to the powerful QuantumScript Optimization Engine to generate the requested optimized resume content, summaries, or cover letters.
  • Tool Persistence: Your input data (but NOT the generated output) is stored securely on your local device's browser storage (LocalStorage) to allow you to save and reload your work between sessions.

3. Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: All resume and job history data is stored exclusively in your browser's LocalStorage. This data is not automatically transferred to our servers or third parties. Clearing your browser's data will erase this saved information.
  • Optimization Engine Transmission: Data sent to the QuantumScript Optimization Engine is transmitted over secure channels (HTTPS) and is used solely for the purpose of generating the requested output. We do not retain or use your personal or job history data submitted via this process for training or for any other commercial purpose.
  • No Server-Side Storage: This tool is designed to operate without a persistent user database. We do not save your input data on our own servers.

4. Cookies and Tracking

This application does not use cookies for tracking or analytics. We only rely on your browser's LocalStorage functionality, which is essential for the basic 'Save Data' and 'Load Saved Data' features.

5. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update this privacy policy at any time. We encourage users to frequently check this page for any changes. By continuing to use the tool, you acknowledge and accept the terms of the updated policy.
